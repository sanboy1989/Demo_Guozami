OUR STORY我們的故事

From the Taiwanese
Kitchen to Your Table 從台灣廚房
到你的餐桌

The story of GUOZAMI begins in a Taiwanese family kitchen. Every dish carries grandma's recipe, mum's technique, and that comforting flavour you never forget. We believe the best food isn't found in Michelin-starred restaurants — it's in that bowl of braised pork rice that makes you feel at home.

GUOZAMI 的故事，從一個台灣家庭的廚房開始。每一道菜，都是外婆的配方、媽媽的手藝，與那個讓人安心的滋味。我們相信，最好的料理不在星級餐廳，而在那一碗讓你想起家的滷肉飯。

We insist on sourcing Taiwanese local ingredients, slow-braised and carefully prepared using traditional techniques, so every bite carries the true warmth of Taiwan. This is the place you'll want to come back to every day.

我們堅持使用台灣本地食材，以傳統技法慢燉細熬，讓每一口都充滿真實的台灣溫度。這裡，是你每天都想回來的地方。
